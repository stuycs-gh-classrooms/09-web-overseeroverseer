Chests and Eggs Map

This is the map for the Chests and Eggs around the map. Chests are an easy source of resources early game or to unlock rare materials and schematics late game. Some might require a key. Eggs are the key to easily progress through the game. With an incubator you unlock many late game pals early on, without needing to wait until level 19 to unlock breeding.

Pal Bosses Location Map

This is the map for the Pal Bosses Location Map around the map. Boss Pals, sometimes also called Alpha Pals, pals that have higher stat values than most other pals around the same area, often being multiple levels higher as well. Some alpha pals are the only way you can optain them, others are just larger versions of the same pal you can catch elsewhere on the map. They are great additions for your team and also drop ancient civilization parts which are needed to progress the ancient technology tree. Early game bosses like Chillet can easily carry you through the early game, while the 5 legendary pals like Paladius are boss pals that you unlock towards the end of the current update.

Fast Travel Map

This is the map for all the Fast Travels on the map. Fast travels are instant teleporters to bring you around the map as soon as you unlock them. A common strategy is respawning multiple times in the beginning of the game at different respawn points to unlock a few distant fast travels right away. They can also be used to teleport to and back from your guild base.
