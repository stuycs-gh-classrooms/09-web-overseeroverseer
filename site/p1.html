    • This is the map for the Pal Bosses Location Map around the map. Boss Pals, sometimes also called Alpha Pals, pals that have higher stat values than most other pals around the same area, often being multiple levels higher as well. Some alpha pals are the only way you can optain them, others are just larger versions of the same pal you can catch elsewhere on the map. They are great additions for your team and also drop ancient civilization parts which are needed to progress the ancient technology tree. Early game bosses like Chillet can easily carry you through the early game, while the 5 legendary pals like Paladius are boss pals that you unlock towards the end of the current update.
